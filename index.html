About Us

Built by students,
for students

NoteNest was created to solve a simple problem — great notes were locked away on personal hard drives while other students struggled to find good study material. We built a platform where knowledge flows freely.

Our mission is to democratise quality education by empowering students to share, discover, and collaborate on study notes across every subject and university.
